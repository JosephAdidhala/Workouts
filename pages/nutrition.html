  • ✅ Water (minimum 500ml on wake)
  • ✅ Black coffee (no cream/sugar)

🍱 Sunday Meal Prep System (2 Hours)

Weekly
Batch Cook List
  • 🍗 4 lbs chicken thighs — season, oven roast (400°F × 35 min)
  • 🍚 6 cups dry rice — rice cooker. Makes ~18 cups cooked.
  • 🍠 6–8 sweet potatoes — bake whole 400°F × 45 min
  • 🥦 2 lbs broccoli — roast with EVOO + turmeric
  • 🥚 12 hard-boiled eggs — boil, refrigerate, grab-and-go
  • 🥩 2 lbs ground turkey — brown with garlic, ginger, spices
Container System
  • 🥡 Portion into 5 lunch containers: protein + rice + veg
  • 🥡 Portion into 5 dinner containers: protein + sweet potato + salad
  • 🧃 Pre-mix: 5 protein shake servings in bags
  • 🫙 Mason jar salads: 5 days of greens
  • 🍎 Pre-cut fruit/veg snacks for grab-and-go
