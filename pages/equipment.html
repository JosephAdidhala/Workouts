These cover 100% of mobility work, 80% of Phase 1 home exercises, and the rehab protocols. Order immediately.

🔗

Resistance Bands Set

~$30

5-band set with loops AND pull-apart bands. Essential for: monster walks, clamshells, band pull-aparts, face pulls, terminal knee extensions, lateral band walks.

Brands: Perform Better, Rogue, Amazon Basics
Get: Loop bands (light/medium/heavy) + long resistance bands
Phase 1 — Week 1
🧻

Foam Roller

~$25

High-density, full length (36"). Used daily for thoracic extension, IT band, hip flexors, quads, calves. Gets harder to skip once you feel the benefit.

Brands: TriggerPoint GRID, Amazon Basics, Yes4All
Avoid: Hollow, lightweight rollers — they compress too easily
Phase 1 — Week 1
🏋️

Doorway Pull-Up Bar

~$35

Non-negotiable. Dead hangs for spinal decompression (every day). Pull-ups starting Week 5. Scapular pull-ups, dead hang shoulder rehab — used in every Phase 1 upper session.

Brands: Iron Gym, Perfect Fitness, Rogue MT
Buy 300lb+ rated — cheap ones fail
Phase 1 — Week 1
🏃

Jump Rope

~$15

Speed rope for future conditioning. Gentle ankle warm-up (Phase 2+, not Week 1 — ankle caution). Light enough for travel. Excellent Zone 2 fasted cardio later.

Brands: CrossRope, WOD Nation
Note: Not for Grade 3 ankle until Month 4+
Phase 2 Ready
⚖️

Digital Food Scale

~$12

The single highest-ROI nutrition purchase. Guessing macros is why most people fail. Weigh everything for 8 weeks — then you'll have accurate intuition forever.

Brands: OXO, Etekcity, Amazon Basics
Get: 0.1g precision, at least 11lb capacity
Day 1

Buy these by Month 3–4. These items dramatically expand what you can do at home — especially on rest days when gym isn't practical.

🏋️

Adjustable Dumbbells (5–52.5 lbs)

~$250–350

Replaces 10+ pairs. Select-a-weight design. Covers DB rows, presses, RDLs, lateral raises, farmer carries, Arnold presses, goblet squats, and more.

Best: Bowflex SelectTech 552, PowerBlock Elite
Budget: Yes4All adjustable (separate plates)
High Priority
💆

Massage Gun

~$80–150

Daily recovery tool. 5–10 minutes on sore muscles dramatically reduces DOMS. Use on glutes, quads, upper traps, calves. Avoid bony areas and spine directly.

Best: Theragun Mini, Hypervolt Go
Budget: Renpho, Bob and Brad models on Amazon
Recovery Game-Changer
🟡

BOSU Ball

~$80–120

Proprioception training — critical for your ankle rehab Phase 2 (Week 8+). Single-leg balance, BOSU squats, push-ups. Replaces wobble board for ankle stability.

Brands: BOSU Pro (commercial), Amazon alternatives
Get: 26" diameter, 300lb+ rated
Ankle Protocol Required
🎯

Gymnastic Rings

~$30–50

Hang anywhere. Extremely versatile — ring rows (great shoulder rehab variation), dips (Phase 2+), push-ups (advanced), muscle-ups later. Portable. Compact. High value.

Brands: Rogue, Nayoya, Yes4All
Get: Wood rings, 1.1" diameter, 2m+ straps
Phase 2 Versatility
⚽

Lacrosse Ball

~$8–15

Deep tissue release for glutes, plantar fascia, pec minor, subscapularis, upper trap, IT band, and thoracic spine (two balls taped together = peanut). Use before mobility work.

Any lacrosse ball works. Get 2 so you can tape them together for spine work.
Buy Now — Cheap
🚴

Rowing Machine

~$300–800

The best home cardio tool for this program. Full body, zero impact, shoulder-safe, posture-reinforcing. 30-min rowing sessions replace all Zone 2 cardio. Concept2 BikeErg also works.

Best: Concept2 Model D (resells for full price)
Budget: LifeFitness RW700, Sunny Health
Significant Purchase

Phase 3–4 level. If you're serious about eliminating gym commute and training at the highest level year-round. Build this over 6–12 months.

🏗️

Power Rack + Barbell + Plates

~$800–1,500

The cornerstone of serious strength training. Squat, bench, deadlift, OHP, pull-ups all from one unit. Buy a 7' barbell and start with 300 lbs of bumper plates.

Best: Rogue RML-390F, Rep PR-4000
Budget: Titan T-3, PowerMark
Phase 3 Goal
🚶

Walking Pad / Under-Desk Treadmill

~$300–600

Walk during meetings and shallow work. +3,000–5,000 steps/day without trying. The passive fat-loss hack. Requires standing desk (or standing laptop riser).

Best: WalkingPad A1 Pro, Urevo
Also get: Monitor/laptop riser for standing desk
NEAT Booster
🧖

Sauna (Infrared)

~$800–2,000

Recovery, cardiovascular health, cortisol reduction, improved sleep. 4× weekly sauna (20 min at 170°F) shows similar cardiovascular benefits to moderate cardio in studies.

Portable: Sauna Space, Relax Sauna (~$500)
Cabin: Sunlighten, Clearlight (higher end)
Longevity Tool
🖥️

Standing Desk (Electric)

~$400–800

Work in alternating positions: seated 45 min, standing 20 min. Essential for the desk protocol. Electric height adjust — manual ones are frustrating and rarely used.

Best: Uplift V2, Flexispot E7
Budget: Flexispot E5, Amazon basics electric
Desk Worker Must-Have
🎯

Cable Machine (Functional Trainer)

~$600–1,200

Replicates cable stack from commercial gym. Face pulls, pallof press, cable rows, pulldowns, triceps, rotator cuff work — all from home. The final piece for shoulder health at home.

Best: REP FT-100, Inspire FT2
Budget: BodyCraft HFT, XMark XM-7626
Shoulder Health
🧊
Ice / Cold Plunge Tub
~$100–3,000

DIY chest freezer (~$200) or dedicated tub. 3–5 min at 50–59°F. 3×/week minimum for serious recovery. Incredible for DOMS and inflammation post-training.

🔴
Red Light Therapy Panel
~$200–500

Photobiomodulation. 10–20 min/day on sore areas + face. Reduces inflammation, improves skin, supports joint recovery. Use AM after mobility work.

🦵
Percussion Massager (Upgrade)
~$250–400

Theragun Pro or Hypervolt 2 Pro — deeper amplitude, better attachments. Worth it if you train 4+ days/week and value recovery.

🩺
Theraband / Floss Bands
~$20–30

Voodoo floss for ankle and knee compression + mobilization. 1–2 min wrapped tight, move through range, remove. Excellent for scar tissue and stiffness.

🍳
Glass Meal Prep Containers (Set of 20)
~$50–70

Store 5 days of food. Microwave safe. BPA-free. No plastic leaching into prepped meals. Costco or Amazon. Get divided containers too.

🥘
Instant Pot / Slow Cooker
~$80–120

Cook 4 lbs of chicken in 15 minutes pressure. Bone broth, stews, rice — hands-off bulk cooking. The meal prep hero.

🥗
High-Speed Blender
~$80–500

Smoothies, protein shakes, sauces. Vitamix lasts 20 years. Nutribullet does 90% of the work for $80. Get what you'll actually use.

💊
Weekly Supplement Organizer
~$10–20

AM/PM compartments for 7 days. Never miss a supplement. Takes 10 min on Sunday to fill. Removes daily cognitive load from supplement protocol.

🏋️ Commercial Gym — What to Prioritize

Keep your gym membership. These are the assets your home setup can't fully replicate (yet):

Essential Gym Equipment
  • 🏋️ Barbell: Deadlift, squat, bench, OHP
  • 🔗 Cable stack: Face pulls, rows, pulldowns
  • 🚴 Cardio equipment: Rower, bike, ski erg
  • 🏊 Pool: Zero-impact Zone 2 (bonus)
Use These on Training Days
  • 📅 Mon/Thu = Lower body + barbell work
  • 📅 Tue/Fri = Upper body + cable machine
  • 📅 Wed/Sat = Home mobility + rower/walk
  • 📅 Sun = Full rest or long walk
