😴 Sunday — Rest + Weekly Review + Prep

Rest Day
📋
Sunday Protocol: Rest is where growth happens. Still do morning mobility stack (10 min) and McGill Big 3.
  • ☑️ 2-hour meal prep session
  • ☑️ Weekly review (see Weekly Review page)
  • ☑️ Log measurements if it's your measurement day
  • ☑️ Plan next week's schedule
  • ☑️ Light 20-min walk if feeling good
  • ☑️ Sleep 8+ hours. In bed by 10 PM.
📈
Block 2 Focus (Days 29–56): You've built the base. Now we add volume, introduce barbell work (deadlift, front squat), increase cardio, and start measurable strength progression. Rehab protocols continue but reduce in time as movement quality improves.

📋 Block 2 Changes

  • ✅ Add barbell Romanian Deadlift
  • ✅ Add Front Squat (or Goblet w/ more weight)
  • ✅ Increase pulling volume (add pull-ups/chin-ups)
  • ✅ Add rowing intervals (2×5 min Zone 3)
  • ✅ Increase carries to 40m
  • ✅ Add DB Shoulder Press (seated)
  • ✅ Zone 2 cardio to 40 min
  • ✅ Track all lifts — progressive overload begins

📊 Progressive Overload Rules

📈
Add weight when you can complete ALL sets with 2 reps in reserve (2 RIR). Add 5 lbs (lower body) or 2.5 lbs (upper body) at a time.
Goblet Squat+5 lbs/wk
Hip Thrust+10 lbs/wk
DB Row+5 lbs/wk
DB Press+5 lbs/wk
RDL (Barbell)+10 lbs/wk
