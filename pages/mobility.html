Mobility Library

Mobility is training. Stiffness is the enemy. Build range — then express strength through it.

⚡
Movement First, Load Second. Pain-free range of motion at every joint is a prerequisite for heavy training. If an area feels tight, stay there — don't rush past it.
🌅

Morning Mobility Stack

10 minutes. Every single day. Before phone, before coffee. This sets the tone.

💡
Do this sequence on the floor immediately after waking. Inhale through nose, exhale through mouth. Move slowly — you're waking your nervous system, not racing.
#ExerciseSets × Reps / TimeFocusVideo
1 90/90 Hip Switch
Sit on floor, both legs bent at 90°. Rotate hips side to side, keeping spine tall.		 2 × 5/side Hip IR/ER mobility ▶ Watch
2 Cat-Cow
On all fours. Max flexion, hold 2 sec. Max extension, hold 2 sec. Breathe into each position.		 1 × 10 reps Spine hydration ▶ Watch
3 Child's Pose with Lateral Reach
Hips to heels. Walk hands left, hold 20 sec. Center, hold 20 sec. Walk right, hold 20 sec.		 1 × 20 sec each Lat / thoracic / hip ▶ Watch
4 Chin Tuck + Neck Rotation
Supine. Chin tuck × 10. Then look full left, hold 3 sec. Full right, hold 3 sec. Repeat ×5.		 2 × 5/side Cervical mobility / FHP fix ▶ Watch
5 World's Greatest Stretch
Lunge position: rotate toward front leg, reach sky with top arm. Hold 3 sec, repeat. Both sides.		 3/side Full body integration ▶ Watch
6 Ankle Circles + Alphabet
Seated. Draw alphabet with big toe for each foot. Then 10 large circles CW, 10 CCW per ankle.		 1 set each ankle Ankle mobility (Grade 3 history) ▶ Watch
7 Thoracic Extension over Foam Roller
Roller perpendicular to spine. Work from mid-back to upper back. 5–7 positions, 3 breaths each.		 3 breaths × 5 spots Thoracic extension (desk posture) ▶ Watch
8 Deep Squat Hold
Feet shoulder width, toes ~30° out. Sink as low as you can, hold. Use pole/door for help initially.		 3 × 20–60 sec Hip / ankle / groin ▶ Watch
📈
Goal Progression: By Week 8 — hold a full depth squat for 60 seconds without support. By Week 16 — perform full depth squat with arms overhead comfortably.
