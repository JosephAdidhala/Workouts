🔁 Lifestyle Rules

  • Minimum effective dose beats burnout
  • Keep bodyweight inside a 5-lb band
  • One weekly review, every week
  • Four anchors never disappear: sleep, protein, steps, mobility
  • Deload every 6–8 weeks automatically

🧭 Expand the Identity

  • Add a sport or recreational skill
  • Rotate physique and performance goals quarterly
  • Travel without losing routine
  • Teach someone else the system
  • Build Year 2 around what you enjoy most
