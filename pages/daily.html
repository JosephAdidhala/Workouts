Daily Operating System

Elite performance is scheduled, not hoped for. This is your hour-by-hour blueprint for a peak day.

🏋️

Training Day Schedule

Monday / Tuesday / Thursday / Friday — the 4 training days. Early morning or evening depending on work. This is the AM-training version.

5:30 AM

🌅 Wake Up Protocol

No phone for 15 min. Drink 500ml water immediately. Open blackout curtains — sunlight triggers cortisol cascade that wakes you fully. Splash cold water on face. Take any AM supplements: D3/K2, Omega-3.

15 min
5:45 AM

🧘 Morning Mobility Stack

10-minute floor mobility sequence. See Mobility Library → Morning. No skipping. This wires your nervous system before the day loads it.

  • 90/90 hip switch
  • Cat-cow × 10
  • Child's pose lateral
  • Chin tuck + neck rotation
  • World's greatest stretch
  • Ankle circles
  • Thoracic foam roll
  • Deep squat hold
10 min
5:55 AM

☕ Caffeine (Optional) + Hydration

Black coffee or black tea only. No sugar, no cream. Hydrate with 400ml water. Creatine 5g in water if taking pre-training. No food — IF window doesn't open until noon.

10 min
6:05 AM

🏋️ Training Session

8-minute targeted warm-up → Main session (50–75 min) → 10-min cool-down. Total: 68–93 min.

Structure
  • 8 min — warm-up activation
  • 50–75 min — main lifts
  • 10 min — cool-down stretching
Rules
  • Log every set in notebook
  • No phone between sets
  • Video your first set to check form
75–95 min
7:40 AM

🚿 Contrast Shower

3 min hot → 30 sec cold → 30 sec hot → 30 sec cold. Ends cold. Reduces DOMS, improves circulation, cortisol regulation. Game-changer for recovery.

10–15 min
8:00 AM

🎯 Deep Work Block 1

Your brain is primed by training. Tackle your most cognitively demanding work now. No meetings if possible. Pomodoro: 50 min work, 10 min break × 2 = 2 hours.

2 hours
10:00 AM

💧 Posture Break + Hydration

Stand up. 2-min posture reset: chin tucks × 10, wall angels × 10, shoulder rolls × 10. Walk 200 steps. Drink 400ml water.

5 min
10:05 AM

🎯 Deep Work Block 2

Second focused block. Email, meetings, collaborative work. Still avoid social media.

2 hours

😴 SLEEP — 7.5 to 9 Hours

Sleep is the most anabolic thing you can do. This is where fat loss and muscle growth actually happen. 7.5 hours minimum. 9 is better.

Sleep Environment
  • Total blackout (blackout curtains)
  • 67–68°F / 19–20°C
  • White noise or silence
  • Phone in another room
Sleep Supplements
  • Magnesium Glycinate 400mg ✅
  • No melatonin (first resort)
  • Tart cherry juice (optional, nighttime)

Desk Worker Protocol

8 hours of sitting will undo training. This system fights back — every 45 minutes, all day.

🧬
Sitting is not neutral. After 30 minutes of uninterrupted sitting: hip flexors tighten, glutes turn off, forward head posture worsens, disc pressure increases. The 45-minute break rule is mandatory for your rehab goals.

⏰ 45-Minute Desk Break Routine

Set a recurring timer every 45 minutes. Stand up immediately when it goes off. Complete this in 3–5 minutes.

🖥️ Ergonomic Desk Setup

  • 📏 Monitor: eye level, arm's length away
  • ⌨️ Keyboard: elbows at 90°, wrists neutral
  • 🪑 Chair: hips slightly above knees, lumbar support
  • 👣 Feet: flat on floor (use footrest if needed)
  • 💡 Lighting: no glare, bright enough
  • 📱 Phone: never held up to ear (use headset)

🪑 Standing Desk Protocol

If you have or get a standing desk, use this rotation:

  • ⏰ 45 min seated → 20 min standing
  • Standing mat required (anti-fatigue)
  • Alternate weight left/right while standing
  • Calf raises during standing calls (add up!)
  • Never stand so long feet hurt — that's also bad
