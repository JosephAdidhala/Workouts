🗓️ 12-Month Phase Roadmap

Months 1–3

🔱 Phase 1: Foundation & Rehab

Days 1–90. Build the base. Fix posture and pain points. Establish habits. Start fat loss.

May 11 – Aug 8, 2026 ~18 lbs to lose 4 days training/week

⚡ Phase 2: Athletic Development

Days 91–180. Higher intensity. More volume. Compound barbell focus. Body recomposition accelerates.

Aug 9 – Nov 6, 2026 Goal: 175 lbs 5 days training/week

🏆 Phase 3: Peak Performance

Days 181–270. Strength + conditioning peak. Advanced programming. Visible physique results prominent.

Nov 7, 2026 – Feb 5, 2027 Goal: 172 lbs 5–6 days training/week

  • Month 9: 9-month progress photos — dramatic transformation visible
Months 10–12

💎 Phase 4: Elite Integration

Days 271–365. Maintenance. Mastery. Lifestyle programming. This is who you are now — not what you're doing.

Feb 6 – May 11, 2027 Goal: Maintain 170–175 4–5 days/week — sustainable
  • Month 10: Design your own 12-week block
  • Month 11: Optional: add sport, martial arts, or recreational training
  • Month 12: 1-year assessment. Plan Year 2.
